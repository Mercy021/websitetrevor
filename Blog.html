BORN A CRIME

-by Trevor Noah

Book summary

Born a crime is the story about a mischievous young boy who grows in a restless young man as he tries to find himself in a world where he was never supposed to exist.His name is Trevor Noah,Trevor was born to a white Swiss father and a Xhosa mother ata a time when such a union was punishable.Being born "a crime" in apartheid South Africa, He reflects on being "half-white, half-black".His mother is a woman who is determined to save him from anything like poverty,violence and abuse.
